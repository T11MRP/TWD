Questions

Before you get in touch.

Is the website mine?

Yes — it's built for your business. The £35/month keeps it hosted, secure and lightly maintained; the site itself is yours.

What if I need more than a minor update?

New pages, a bigger redesign, or added features (like online booking) are quoted separately — just message me and I'll give you a price.

Can I use a domain I already own?

Yes. If you've already got one, that's fine — the domain allowance just isn't needed in that case.

How long does it take?

Usually just a matter of days once we've had a chat about the business and I've got a few photos to work with.
